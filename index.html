#

Light My Scene

Turn any screen into a photography light source. Pick a solid color, dial in a custom hex, or run animated lighting effects -- all from your browser.

  • Tap a color swatch for instant background change
  • Enter a hex code or use the color picker for any hue
  • Try Police, Fire, or Storm for animated lighting FX
  • Adjust intensity and speed to match your shot
  • Hit "Hide Controls" to go fullscreen -- tap anywhere to bring them back

Built for photographers and content creators who need quick, free colored lighting on an iPad or any screen.
